Frequently Asked Questions

Can I schedule emails to send later?

Yes, Dmailer allows you to schedule your emails to be sent at a later date and time.

What file formats are accepted for the resume attachment?

You can upload PDFs, DOC, or DOCX files as your resume to attach with emails.

Is my Gmail password stored anywhere?

No, your Gmail app password is only used during the current session to send emails and is not stored.

Can I preview the email before sending?

Absolutely! Use the 'Preview Email' option on the mailing page before scheduling bulk sending.
